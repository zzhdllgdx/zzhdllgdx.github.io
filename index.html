SHORT BIOGRAPHY Dr. Naigao Jin is an Assistant Professor with School of Software at Dalian University of Technology (DUT).
A visiting scholar hosted by Prof. Tian He at UMN from Jan. 2012 to Jan. 2013. He received his Ph.D. degree in Signal Processing from Dalian University of Technology in 2008. Naigao obtained B.E. and M.E. degrees from Dalian Jiaotong University and DUT in 1999 and 2002, respectively. Naigao is currently a member of IEEE Computer Society.
RESEARCH INTERESTS I enjoy building embedded systems and have a strong passion for wireless sensor networking, audio signal processing. Currently, my research theme is smartphone computing including but not limited to the following topics:
➤ Time synchronization, localization and tracking in wireless sensor networks.
➤ Indoor localization based on Smartphone
➤ Localization in underwater sensor network.
